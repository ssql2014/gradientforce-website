Research Directions

AI for IP Design

Revolutionizing semiconductor IP development with AI-powered design tools. We deliver industry-leading PPA (Power, Performance, Area) through intelligent automation.

  • AI-assisted RTL design and optimization
  • Automated verification and test generation
  • AI-driven synthesis and place-and-route
  • Open-source IP customization (e.g., Coral NPU)

AI for Engineering Services

Empowering customer projects with AI-enhanced engineering support. We tackle the most difficult technical challenges using cutting-edge AI methodologies.

  • AI-powered debug and root cause analysis
  • Intelligent design optimization consulting
  • Automated documentation generation
  • Complex problem solving with AI assistance

Self-Evolving Hardware

Pioneering hardware-software co-design for neural networks that can self-train and evolve. Creating adaptive circuits for next-generation computing.

  • Self-learning FPGA architectures
  • Adaptive CPU designs
  • Hardware-software co-optimization
  • Neuromorphic computing research

Silicon Photonics Inverse Design

Applying AI inverse design techniques to silicon photonics, enabling next-generation optical communication solutions for data centers.

  • AI-driven photonic device optimization
  • High-bandwidth optical interconnects
  • Data center communication solutions
  • Novel photonic component design

About Gradient Force

Gradient Force is an R&D driven innovation center headquartered in Hong Kong, dedicated to solving complex engineering challenges through deep AI research.

We believe that AI is not just a tool, but a fundamental paradigm shift in how engineering problems are approached and solved. Our mission is to bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI capabilities and real-world engineering applications.

From semiconductor IP design to silicon photonics, our team combines deep expertise in AI/ML, VLSI design, and systems engineering to push the boundaries of what's possible.
